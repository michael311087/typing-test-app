How to Take the Test

  • Select your difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  • Click "Start Test" to begin.
  • Type the displayed text as quickly and accurately as possible.
  • Your speed (WPM) and accuracy will be shown after the test.
  • Track your progress with session statistics and best scores.
UK English tip: practice is the noun, practise is the verb.
